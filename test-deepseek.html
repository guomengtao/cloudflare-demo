Details of Disappearance

Sa'Wade was last seen at her family's residence in Garryowen, Montana, on the Crow Indian Reservation, after 11:00 p.m. on October 6, 2024. When her family came to wake her the next morning, she was gone. An extensive search of the local area turned up no sign of her and she has never been heard from again.

Sa'Wade left her phone behind and her family gave it to law enforcement for analysis, but they were unable to glean any information from it. She has no history of running away or getting into serious trouble, and her loved ones stated she was thriving in school at the time of her disappearance. The circumstances of Sa'Wade's disappearance are unclear.
