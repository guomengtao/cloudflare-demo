Missing

Native American male. Black hair, brown eyes. Baker is an enrolled member of the Navajo Nation. The left side of his bottom lip is pierced. He wears eyeglasses with brown frames. Tattoos on both forearms and hand.

Gray t-shirt, blue sweatpants, sneakers, two turquoise rings on left ring finger, camouflage-print hat with neck flap.

Details of Disappearance

Baker was last seen in Pinon, Arizona on July 18, 2022. He has never been heard from again. Few details are available in his case, but he is known to have strong ties to the Navajo Nation community.
