Details of Disappearance

Abbott was last seen on Grand Louis Road in Inchelium, Washington at 9:00 a.m. on March 27, 2017. She left her home on Grand Louis Road with her dog, a tan and gray pit bull named Jay, to check the mail, and never returned. She has never been heard from again. A photo of Jay is posted with this case summary.

Because of her Alzheimer's Disease, Abbott may believe she lives in Spokane, Washington or Sonoma, California, places she used to live, and may try to travel back to either of those places. Her case remains unsolved.
