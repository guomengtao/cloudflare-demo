Tonia Gay Aldrich

Missing Since: March 29, 1997

From: Elyria, Ohio

Case Summary

Tonia Gay Aldrich was last seen on March 29, 1997, at Chris's Place, a bar on Clark Street in Elyria, Ohio. She was 38 years old at the time of her disappearance.

Aldrich was described as a white female, 5'0" tall, weighing 110 pounds, with brown hair and brown eyes. She had a tattoo of a panda bear on her right shoulder and may have had a small tattoo of a star on her left shoulder.

Foul play is suspected in her disappearance.

Investigation

The Elyria Police Department is investigating Aldrich's disappearance. If you have any information, please contact them at 440-326-1205 or 440-323-3302.
