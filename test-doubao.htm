Physical Characteristics

Native American female. Brown hair, brown eyes. Sa'Wade's hair was shoulder-length at the time of her disappearance. She is an enrolled member of the Mandan, Hidatsa and Arikara Nations and of Crow and Hidatsa heritage.

Clothing & Items

A black hooded sweatshirt with mushrooms on it, an anime themed t-shirt, basketball shorts, ankle socks, purple slip-on Sketchers shoes and possibly an elk tooth necklace. Possibly carrying a black and purple backpack with a green Adidas logo.

Sa'Wade has autism and a learning disability.
